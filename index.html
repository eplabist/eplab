Engineering Physics Laboratory/epLab

The epLab provides a comprehensive foundation in experimental physics for undergraduate engineering students. Our facility is equipped with precision instruments that allow learners to explore core physical principles through hands-on investigation. Students engage in experiments that strengthen analytical thinking, technical skills, and scientific methodology. Through structured experimentation and guided learning, we aim to cultivate a deep appreciation for the physical laws governing engineering systems.
